Civic Education

Vote Africa provides the electorate will all relevant educational material which is accessible offline and aims to reduce the rate of invalid votes and motivate electorate to participate.

  • Voter GuideOffline Virtual Library, Info Guide, Useful Links & Resources
  • Polling StationsGet directions to the nearest polling station in your area
  • Open DataStatistics on past African elections for research and analysis
  • Representing CandidatesGet to know all the representing candidates participating during the elections.
  • Political PartiesGet all relevant information about parties running for the elections
  • Live ResultsReceive election results in real time.
